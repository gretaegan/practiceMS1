Why Choose Us?

Our Team

Here at Wings Flight Training Academy we pride ourselves on the expertise of our staff. Our instructors are all former or current airline and military pilots with real-life jet and turbo-prop experience. Our fleet of aircraft are expertly maintained by our 8 highly skilled engineers.

Our Fleet

Here at Wings, our training up to PPL standard is conducted on our fleet of 10 Cessna 152 single-engined piston aircraft. Considered a stable, safe and enjoyable aircraft to fly, this two-seater will take you from your first flight to your solo cross-country navigation adventures.

Once students have reached the 50hour mark they will upgrade to the 4-seater Cessna 172, the big brother of the 152. This aircraft is slightly larger, heavier and can hold more fuel enabaling the pilot to fly further afield.

The CPL training and CPL Skills tests are flown on our C172RG aircraft, EI-FII, which is a conventional 172 with a retractable landing gear and a variable pitch propeller.

The ME/IR training is flown on our fleet of 3 Beechcraft 76 twin-engined piston aircraft. Students will learn the skills necessary to fly multi-engined aircraft during normal flight as well as engine failure situations.

Finally, the APS/MCC training will be conducted on our Boeing737-800 simulator. This simulator is fully certified within EASA standards and is full motion, so feels like you are flying the real thing.
