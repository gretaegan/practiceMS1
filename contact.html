Entry Requirements for Integrated Course 2021 :

Prospective students must have the following:

  • Be at least 18 years of age prior to the starting date
  • Be in possession of a Class 1 Medical Certificate
  • Be proficient in mathematics and at least one science subject

If entry requirements are satisfied, you will be contacted by phone or email to arrange an assessment and interview at our training facility in Weston Airport. Successful applicants will be offered a place on the course on completion
